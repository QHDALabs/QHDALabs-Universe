# QHDALabs-Universe Manifesto

We live in the age of precision cosmology.

Yet precision without audited assumptions can become illusion.

We believe:

- data pipelines matter
- uncertainty must be modeled honestly
- observation is a system
- reproducibility is non-negotiable
- bold theory must meet measurable reality

We do not oppose science.

We strengthen it.

Before rewriting the Universe,
we validate how it is measured.
